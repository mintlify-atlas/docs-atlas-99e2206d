---
title: "Slack integration"
description: "Receive Probo compliance notifications in Slack"
---

The Slack integration sends compliance event notifications directly to channels in your Slack workspace. When a task is assigned, an evidence request comes in, or a document needs approval, the right people get notified in Slack without needing to check Probo manually.

## What Probo notifies about

- **Task assignments** — when a compliance task is assigned to a team member
- **Evidence requests** — when evidence is requested for a measure or control
- **Document approvals** — when a document version is submitted for approval or has been approved

## Setting up the Slack integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open integration settings">
    In Probo, navigate to **Settings → Integrations → Slack**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the OAuth flow">
    Click **Connect to Slack**. You will be redirected to Slack's authorization page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Probo">
    Select the Slack workspace you want to connect and click **Allow**. Probo requests only the permissions it needs to post messages to channels.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select notification channels">
    After authorization, return to the Probo settings page and choose which Slack channel should receive each type of notification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to activate the integration. Probo will send a test message to confirm the connection is working.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You need to be a Slack workspace admin (or have permission to install apps) to complete the OAuth flow.
</Note>

## Disconnecting Slack

To remove the Slack integration:

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Slack**.
2. Click **Disconnect**.
3. Confirm the removal.

Probo will immediately stop sending notifications to Slack. If you want to revoke Probo's access from the Slack side as well, go to your Slack workspace settings under **Apps** and remove Probo from there.
